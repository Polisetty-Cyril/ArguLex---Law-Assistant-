About Legal Chatbot

Our Mission

Legal Chatbot aims to make legal assistance more accessible and efficient through advanced AI technology. We provide instant document analysis and legal guidance to help users better understand their legal documents and questions.

Features
  • Document Analysis: Upload and analyze legal documents with AI-powered insights
  • Legal Chat: Get instant answers to your legal questions
  • Professional Mode: Advanced analysis for legal professionals
  • General Mode: Simplified explanations for everyday legal questions
How It Works
  1. Choose between professional or general chat mode
  2. Upload documents or ask questions directly
  3. Receive instant analysis and answers
  4. Download reports and save chat history
